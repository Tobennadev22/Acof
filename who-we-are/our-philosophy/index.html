Our Mission:
Building member's success

ACOF is a Group of Gentlemen committed to each others success and providing a circle of support that members can leverage on in dealing with all of life's challenges

6.8

By Bonding beyond bothers, ACOF's Web of friendship spreads across the Globe

— Our Goals

To support each other in times of celebration or needs

To lead a positive change in Afikpo and the world at large

To influence policies and public opinion in the interest of the common man

Our vision

A Better society with a high sense of values and mutual respect

— Since 2006 : Our History

In April 2006, Ekuma Elem (Bongos) wedded his wife Rose in an occasion that brought a lot of old friends together. That solidarity became movement and that movement became the Afikpo Committee of Friends
